👋 Welcome to KanyeGuess!

Ready to test your Yeezy knowledge? Start by reading the lyrics on screen and guessing the song title by typing it in the text box, then hit “Submit” or press Enter.

If you're stumped, you can skip to the next song by pressing the “Skip” button or entering a dash (-). Remember, you have only 10 skips and 5 lives.

Every incorrect answer costs a life, and if you lose all your lives, well, it’s game over!

Enjoy KanyeGuess and have fun testing your Yeezy knowledge! ✌️
